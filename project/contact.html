Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?
You can schedule a consultation by visiting the "Schedule Consultations" section on our homepage and following the prompts.
Can I upload my prescription?
Yes, you can upload your prescription during the consultation booking process or through your account dashboard.
How do I get medications delivered?
We partner with pharmacies to deliver medications to your home. You can select this option during your consultation.
Where can I find health resources?
You can find health resources and blogs in the "Health Blogs" section of our website.
