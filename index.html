Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic

The Dominican Republic is the Caribbean's most visited destination, known for
stunning beaches, mountains, and rich culture. Santo Domingo, its capital, is
home to the first cathedral and university in the Americas. The country is the
birthplace of merengue and bachata, iconic musical styles loved worldwide. It
features incredible natural contrasts, from Pico Duarte, the Caribbean's highest
peak, to Lake Enriquillo, its lowest point.
